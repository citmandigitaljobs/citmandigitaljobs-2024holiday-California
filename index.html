California

Los Angeles

County of Los Angeles

Landing in LA, we finally set foot together on US soil. After tipping 100% for a cup of coffee, we made our way to Union Station. A short walk around the block (to the shock of the locals, who don't walk anywhere), we found a T Mobil with super helpful staff who set us up with a US SIM card and cell phone number.

We made our way back to the LA Amtrak lounge and after charging our phones, and recharging ourselves with a short doze, we boarded the South West Chief to Flagstaff, Arizona.

Amtrak and a quick look at LA

San Francisco

San Francisco County

Arriving at Emeryville train station from Granby, we caught the bus and rode over the grey Bay Bridge (which we thought was the Golden Gate Bridge, but wasn't) to San Francisco. What a delightful city! Trolley cars, walking up and down the steep roads like we've seen in the movies, self-driving cars and our special diner. And then there was Pier 14, Alcatraz, Fisherman's Wharf, a magnificent meatloaf sandwich, gorgeous buildings, the little kid who wanted to keep you, street names imprinted on the footpaths, fog, and more visits to the diner.
